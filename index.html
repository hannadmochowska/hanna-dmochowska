Neuroscience × AI in Medicine

I’m a researcher working at the intersection of neuroscience and AI. I’m finishing an MSc in AI in Medicine at UCD (’26), after an honours BSc in Biomedical Science & Neuroscience at AUC. I’ve spent the last few years moving between Denmark, Amsterdam, and now Dublin—studying the brain, working on memory and sleep at the Genzel Lab, and learning how machine learning can actually support medicine. A lot of my work comes back to the same question: how can we use technology to understand how the brain learns, remembers, and changes? I’m interested in research that’s clear, practical, and genuinely helpful—work that can go beyond theory and make a real difference for memory, health, and everyday life.
