Neuroscience × AI in Medicine

I’ve followed curiosity across Europe—studying Biomedical Science & Neuroscience in Amsterdam @AUC, working on memory and sleep at the Genzel Lab, and now exploring AI in Medicine in Dublin @UCD. Along the way I’ve become obsessed with one question: how can we use technology to understand how the brain learns, remembers, and changes? At the intersection of neuroscience and machine learning, I’m drawn to work that is both rigorous and useful—closed-loop sleep experiments, clean EEG/MEG analyses, and tools others can actually run. I care about building methods that move from paper to practice, with an eye on real impact for memory, health, and everyday life.
